This app was created to help aspiring investment bankers prepare for their interviews. All these questions and solutions have been taken from the Breaking Into Wall Street guide.


To get started, open the side menu and select your desired topic of interest. The number in parentheses indicates the number of possible questions for that topic. After selecting a topic, a question will automatically be generated for you. You can click the "Solution" button to view the suggested answer or click the "Next Question" button to generate another question.


Good luck with your interviews!






*** Due to the nature of the app, you may see the same question appear occasionally. Just keep hitting the "Next Question" button to cycle through them all.
